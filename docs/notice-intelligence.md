# Notice Intelligence

## Purpose

Notice Intelligence helps users quickly understand government notices, payroll tax correspondence, compliance letters, and agency communications.

## Problem

Organizations receive large volumes of notices and often struggle to determine:

- Who sent the notice
- What action is required
- Due dates
- Potential penalties
- Escalation requirements

## Solution

The system analyzes notice content and identifies:

- Agency
- Notice type
- Due date
- Required action
- Priority level
- Risk level

## Example

Input:

State Tax Notice

Output:

Agency: State Tax Department

Priority: High

Action Required: Respond within 15 days

Risk Level: Medium
