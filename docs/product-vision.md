# Product Vision

## Mission

Create an AI-powered workflow intelligence platform that helps individuals and organizations organize information, prioritize actions, retain context, and receive intelligent recommendations.

## Problem

Modern work is fragmented across emails, documents, spreadsheets, notes, chats, and tasks.

People lose context, miss deadlines, forget commitments, and spend significant time deciding what to do next.

## Solution

A workflow intelligence platform that serves as a centralized operational memory system.

The platform helps users:

- Capture information
- Organize projects
- Prioritize actions
- Retain context
- Receive AI-assisted recommendations
- Reduce operational overwhelm

## Core Features

### Context Memory

Retain important information across projects, tasks, and conversations.

### Intelligent Prioritization

Recommend next-best actions based on urgency, deadlines, and business impact.

### Workflow Intelligence

Organize work into actionable workflows.

### Operational Insights

Identify bottlenecks, risks, trends, and opportunities.

## Target Users

- Professionals
- Managers
- Business Owners
- Consultants
- Operations Teams
- Project Teams

## Long-Term Vision

Build an intelligent operating system for work that reduces mental overhead and improves decision-making.
