# User Personas

## Busy Professional

### Challenges
- Too many tasks
- Information overload
- Missed follow-ups

### Goals
- Prioritize work effectively
- Reduce mental clutter
- Improve productivity

---

## Business Owner

### Challenges
- Managing multiple projects
- Tracking decisions
- Delegating work

### Goals
- Better visibility
- Faster decision making
- Workflow automation

---

## Knowledge Worker

### Challenges
- Context switching
- Lost information
- Disorganized notes

### Goals
- Retain context
- Organize information
- Improve execution

---

## Student / Researcher

### Challenges
- Managing assignments
- Tracking research
- Organizing information

### Goals
- Better planning
- Improved focus
- Knowledge management
